Guitar

At the start of 2019, I was watching the Youtube when I stumbled upon the channel Music Is Win hosted by Tyler Lawson. I thought that he looked awesome playing is green PRS guitar and had to have one. So for christmas my brother and I got an acoustic guitar and an electric guitar. Two or three years later of pretending to know how to play, we got a guitar teacher to show us how to actually play. We were taught music theory, the thing that almost every guitars jokes about not needing to learn. Not long after, my brother and I started playing guitar for our church. Although stopping not long after it did teach how to play live in front of people, like more than 100 people. Not long after leaving the church group, we played live blues on Wednesday nights. Teaching us improvisation when playing guitar. Since we initially got the guitars I have been playing for about 5 or 6 six years now, and yes of course im better than my brother.
